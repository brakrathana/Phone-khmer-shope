About Phone Khmer Shop

Your trusted smartphone partner in Cambodia since 2018

Our Story

Phone Khmer Shop was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to provide Cambodian consumers with access to genuine, high-quality smartphones at affordable prices. What started as a small shop in Phnom Penh has grown into one of the most trusted smartphone retailers in Cambodia.

We understand that a smartphone is more than just a device - it's an essential tool for communication, work, entertainment, and connection. That's why we carefully select every product we sell, ensuring it meets our high standards for quality, performance, and value.

Our Mission

To make premium technology accessible to everyone in Cambodia through authentic products, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

To become Cambodia's most trusted consumer electronics retailer, recognized for quality, reliability, and innovation.
