Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer warranty on your products?

Yes, all our products come with official warranty from the manufacturers or authorized distributors. Warranty periods vary by brand and product, typically ranging from 12 to 24 months.

What is your delivery time?

For Phnom Penh, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 3 PM. For other provinces, delivery typically takes 2-3 business days via trusted courier services.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes, we have a 7-day return policy for defective products. Exchanges are subject to product availability. Please keep the original packaging and all accessories for returns.

Do you offer installment payment options?

Yes, we partner with several banks and financial institutions to offer installment plans. Please contact our sales team for details about available payment plans.
